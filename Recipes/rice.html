Rice

The ultimate side dish, feeding 20% of the worlds population makes it the most poular carb

Ingredients

  • White Rice
  • Water
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice in cold water and then strain until water is clear-ish
  2. Combine 1 part rice to 2 parts water (I usually do 180g to 2 cups water)
  3. add rice into water
  4. Add desired salt amount to water (1 Tsp to every 180g of rice, tastes good without soy sauce)
  5. Bring to a boil and simmer for 17-20 min
  6. Test by eating a small amount of rice, should be a little chewy not soft and mushy
  7. At this point the water should be mostly evaporated
  8. Remove from heat and let sit for 5-10 min, letting the rice fully absorb all of the water
