Sweet and Sour Sauce

A very simple sauce used for many types of dishes, most popular being asian food

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup White Sugar
  • 2/3 Cup Water
  • 1/3 Cup Vinegar
  • 1/4 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 2 TBSP Cornstarch
  • 1 TBSP Ketchup

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients besides cornstarch into pot and stir on medium heat until boiling
  2. Add some water to cornstarch and mix until no chunks remain
  3. Add to pot and simmer for 1 minute or until thickened
  4. Cool and serve
