Home Fries

Classic fries, goes great with hot dogs, burgers, or just on their own

Ingredients

  • 400g-1kg Any style potato
  • 1 TBSP Oil of your choice (something with a higher smoke point like peanut oil is best)
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • [Optional] Various seasonings like oregano, thyme, paprika or chili powder add flavour too!

Instructions

  1. Slice all potatoes into whatever uniform shape you'd like (cubed, shoestring, wedges etc.)
  2. simmer in a pot of water on low to low-medium for a few minutes, checking frequently
  3. stick a knife or fork through, should retain its shape but give without crumbling like cold butter
  4. strain and toss in a bowl with the oil and salt until combined
  5. Ideally lay them out on a baking pan lined with wax paper or foil, deeper pans work too
  6. Bake @ 400F for 30 min, flipping halfway, works in air fryer too
  7. let stand for 5 minutes and serve
